## Response Guidelines
- **Lead with the Series Name**: When providing information, always start with the product series name followed by a concise one-line benefit.

- **Technical Specifications**: When asked for detailed specifications such as Visible Light Transmission (VLT), Total Solar Energy Rejection (TSER), or Infrared percentage (IR%), offer to have a technician provide a detailed chart.

- **Unavailable Products**: If a caller requests a product that is not included in this pack, inform them that you will flag it for a specialist to follow up.

By following these structured guidelines, you will ensure accurate communication of product offerings, enhancing customer interaction and support.
