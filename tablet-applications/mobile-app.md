Mobile App (Android and IOS capable)

Online Video

Watch the video for an overview of the mobile app. Note no sound with video. Description written on screen.

http://www.newlineasp.com/AuctionSoftware/AdditionalModules/CRMApplicationforMobileDevices.aspx

Android

The app icon on your mobile phone looks like this.

Click the icon. The Newline ASP splash screen appears whilst the app loads.

Main Menu

The Main menu has four options.

Search Contacts

Search by Name or account number

Allows the user to search for customers (sellers and or buyers) from the main HQ system database. Click the search icon and enter the customer name or account number. The search works the same as in NAS and the user can use a # to split the surname and first name if they wish.

A list of matching results is displayed. Select the one you require.

Search by address \ town

You can enter a town and search for all customers in that town or area.

Add Site Visit Notes

Site Notes at HQ

By Customer

Double click to see contact detail and mark as follow-up or completed.

Contact Management

Contact and Stock Analysis from history

Select the options you want to analysis by. Seller or Buyer. Contact Dates ranges, site and section selections. Tick Show No of Contacts. The results will show how much stock the seller or buyer has had in the date range and how many contacts have been made by reps during that time.

View Sale History

If you have a mobile signal and connection to the HQ server you can access history for sellers or buyers.

This gives the fieldsman chapter and verse of specific trading history.

Make a phone call

Lets the user call the customer and records that the call has been made.

Send a text

Lets the user text the customer.

See customers nearby

If the customer account has a postcode the search icon is available on the mobile app. It will search for other customers close by. This gives the rep an idea of who else they could go and visit whilst close by.

Click the google maps icon in the top right to view on a map.
