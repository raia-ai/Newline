Charges – Buyer Invoices

Add a charge in buyer invoices

To add a charge to a buyer invoice select I for invoice and D for charges.

This loads the charge screen.

Select the charge description.

Select the Charge By method from the drop-down combo and then populate the rate and calculation variables depending on the charge by method selected.

See charges by tutorial for further information.

Example

User selected by head so they see the number of head on the lots and add a rate to multiply by. Click apply when form is complete.

Paid To

If the charge is to be added to the invoice and paid to another party search for the other party name in the paid to box.

Enter part of the truckers name and press enter to see matching results.

Select the one you want.

On clicking apply see a summary of what you have selected.

Click the close button

Details are added to the invoice.

Drill down on charges

Double click the i for information on how that charge is calculated.

Grouping

Groups of charges can be set up and a processing group allows users to create a processing list for the yard staff to apply meds and treatment to animals before they leave the market for feedlots.

In the charges form see the group column. Select charges from the processing group and add them to the buyers invoice. These charges may have default rates in which case the rate will be pre-populated for you.

The processing charge is grouped on the invoice.

This can be ungrouped in the option menu. Untick show charge groups.

The i icon can be clicked to show the drill down breakdowns.

A processing worksheet is produced for the yard team.

Copies can be found in the copy invoice screen.
