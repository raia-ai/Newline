The VAT3 Return in Ireland looks like this:

To find figures in NAS to complete the VAT Return go to the following menu.

Enter the VAT return date range.

click continue

The report shows the gross hammer price during the date range selected. In this example.

To calculate the ex VAT element divide the gross total by 1.048

3122507.25 / 1.048 = 2979491.60 ex VAT

The VAT element of this is the difference between the gross and ex VAT figures

3122507.25 â€“ 2979491.60 = 143015.60

The top up VAT is the ex VAT 2979491.60 * 0.2% = 5958.98

There may be a slight difference due to rounding calculations are done per lot in Newline and not on the gross value. The List item is the top up VAT figure. This is part of your VAT on Purchases total.

The VAT on charges figure will be part of your VAT on Sales total.

If you trade with NI or UK buyers you will have a VAT refund charge (or a similar named charge). This gives the buyer the VAT back on exported items.

This is part of your VAT on Purchases total.

It is possible to get the total for goods to other EU countries through the stock analysis part of NAS.

Tick buyer

Click All Offices & All Sections

Tick the group that you use for zero rate / export VAT. It may have a different name (it is user definable).

Click run analysis

Bottom right shows you the total of goods to EU countries
