NAS is an extremely flexible system that may be configured to suit client requirements and operations. Questions to be asked:

Sale sites

Sale days

Frequency of sales

Stock type groups

Item types

Commission rates

Charges

Number of users

Printouts

Company logo jpg

T&Cs

Accounts package used

How many sites do you operate sales from?

What sale days do you operate?

What is the frequency of sales?

What stock type groups do you sell (you may need to explain what you mean by stock type groups)?

What item types do you sell within these groups (note you may need to explain what you mean by item types)?

Which day do you sell them on?

What seller commission rates do you charge?

What buyer commission rates do you charge?

What other charges do you use?

How many users will you need?

What are their names?

Do you have pre-printed stationary or would you prefer plain paper with a jpg printed on it?

May we have Company graphics jpg?

Company T&Cs

Company TAX number

Printouts

How many copies of the buyer invoice do you require?

Do you wish to use different colour paper for passouts?

What accounts package do you use?
