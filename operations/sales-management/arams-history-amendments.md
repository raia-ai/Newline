To amend sheep movement history to ARAMs \ South Western after the sale has been closed in NAS go to the History Menu.

Select the EID History option.

Select which country movements you wish to amend.

Select the year of movements. It will default to the current year. Click ok.

Double click the sale you want to view or make amendments in.

Select if you want to view on movements or off movements.

Click the row you wish to amend and then right click to get the amendment form.

Enter the new holding number or new postcode and click the send button.
