Transfer Sales from Cardigan to Begin a Sale

Call Wendy @ Cardigan and ask if she has  set  it  up  for  the  right  week
number and date.

Double click the icon

[pic]

Click the File Transfer option, top left of the screen.

[pic]

This will open the following screen

[pic]

The sale will be in the download.zip file shown on the right  hand  side  of
the screen. You must click it once to highlight it. Check that  it  has  the
correct date  by  scrolling  right.  You  will  be  copying  the  file  from
c:\download  at  the  Cardigan  side  to  d:\download  at  the  remote  side
(Fishguard or Haverfordwest).

Click the blue arrow pointing left toward laptop2 to copy  the  download.zip
file from Cardigan to the remote site.

[pic]

[pic]

Click yes.

[pic]

Click yes. At the bottom of the screen it will  say  command  complete  when
the file is copied. Again check the file date on the left hand side  of  the
screen.

[pic]

Click the End Session  option  on  the  left  hand  menu.  Confirm  the  end
session.

[pic]

Click the Sale2 on Laptop2 icon if you are at Fishguard  and  the  Sale1  on
Laptop1 icon if you are at Haverfordwest.

[pic]


Enter 1 for option Receive Central Market data.

[pic]

Enter M for modem. In the blue screens everything needs  to  be  entered  in
UPPERCASE/CAPITALS.

[pic]

[pic]

Enter the sale name you are updating, either sale1  or  sale2.  Enter  C  to
continue.

[pic]

You are asked how you want to make the customer index. Select  F  to  search
for customers by surname and 1st line of address.

Exit out of the system. You should  notice  that  the  sale  date  and  week
number are now up-to-date and the lot count set back to 0 ready to  begin  a
sale.

Transfer Sales Back to Cardigan

Click the MovemySQL icon

[pic]

Click the Copy Data to Main Server button.

[pic]
Click copy sale.

[pic]

Update complete will appear.

Click the sale icon. Select the option 3.

[pic]

[pic]

Enter the sale name to send back to Caridgan. Enter C to continue and type
GO. Enter M for modem.

Double click the icon

[pic]

Click the File Transfer option, top left of the screen.

[pic]

This Time you are copying the file from the laptop at Fishguard or
Haverfordwest to Cardigan so select the download.zip file on the left hand
side of the screen so it goes blue and click the Yellow arrow pointing to
the right.

[pic]

Answer yes to the questions to replace the file. Let Wendy know you have
sent the sale back.
