Create Catalogue in Word

In the During the Sale Menu – Sale Sheets Option, there is a  sub  option  –
Create Catalogue in Word. If you pre-book stock this facility  is  available
to you.

[pic]

Tick the relevant section and click the Continue button.

[pic]

Tick the options you want and click  View  Catalogue.  You  must  have  Word
installed on the PC for this to work.
