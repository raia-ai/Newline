Apply Interest in the sales ledger to overdue accounts:

Once a month select calculate interest.

Enter the date ranges you wish to apply interest charges over. The to date will be 14 or 15 days back from today.

Enter the annual percentage charge to apply.

Save and apply the charges by clicking save and update ledger.

Calc:

((No of days of debt * % rate) * value of debt / 100) / 365

Example

((23 days debt * 5) * 714 / 100) /365 = 2.25

The old QB interest program is replaced by one in the menu of the aged debt list option.

Enter the date to calculate the interest to. Top left.

Enter the no of days grace and this will auto populate the charge interest to date field.

Enter the interest from date.

You can override the no of days debt used in the interest formula by entering 31 days for example in the no of days debt override text box.

Click Calc button.
