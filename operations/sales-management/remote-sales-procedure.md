      User name:REMOTE
      Password:  LETMEIN

    • Option 4:   Download Sale to Remote Server


      [pic]
    • Select relevant sale from list
    • Click on continue.

The sale is now copied across to the laptop server.

Note: it is always advised to enter and sell a dummy lot not on the network
whilst still in the office.
