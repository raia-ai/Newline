iBidder provides an app called Toolbox. From this you can download files which may imported directly into NAS saving you time. You do not have to retype buyers or prices.

This options lets you download all registered buyers to a file called: IB-bidders-7135.tab

(NB 7135 is unique sale no and varies from sale to sale)

This option lets you download the prices and buyers. IB-results-7135.tab
