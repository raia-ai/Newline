Movelot is a multi-function program. It is used for:

   1. Moving lots from one sale to another

   2. Moving lots from one section to another and changing the itemtype
      within the same sale.

   3. Moving lots from one section to another and changing the lot numbers
      within the same sale.

   4. Producing Salesheets grouped by number of animals in a batch

   5. Producing skeleton salesheets with basic vendor, lot and number of hds
      info.
