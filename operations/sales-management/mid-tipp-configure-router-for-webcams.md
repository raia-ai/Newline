               Configure Broadband Router for Webcams.
               1. Open Router Shortcut located on the desktop ‘Router Config’

               2. Login using the credentials provided

               3. Once logged in Click on NAT , Located on the left hand side. Then click Open Ports




               4. Select the camera you wish to alter.




                    Click Here to                      Camera currently
                        select                    Broadcasting will have WAN1

               5. Once you have selected Index 1 or 2 you need to tick/untick Enable Open Ports to the relevent
                  camera. You will need to select the other camera and do the alternate.




               6. Click OK to save changes then close Internet Explorer Window.
