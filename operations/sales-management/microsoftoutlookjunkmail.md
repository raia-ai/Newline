                     Microsoft Outlook:     Junk E-mail

To initialize the Junk E-mail facility click on;

Tools

Organise

Junk E-mail

This will open a window above your inbox, click on;

Junk E-mail

Select folder that you want junk e-mail to go to, it should defult  to  junk
e-mail.
Select the move or colour junk e-mail and the move or colour adult  material
e-mail.
Click on the turn on button to activate.

When junk e-mail arrives right click on it and select send to  junk  e-mail.
Once you have done this once for a  selected  e-mail  address  that  address
will always be sent to junk e-mail.

If you want to view all address in the junk e-mail folder, click on;

Tools

Organise

Junk E-mail

Click here (for more options)

Edit junk senders

There are other methods of achieving the same result, this seems to  be  the
most straight forward.
