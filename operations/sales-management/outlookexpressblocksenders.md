[pic]
                  Microsoft Outlook Express:   Block Sender

When an e-mail comes in and you want to block it, click on;

Message

Block Sender

A message window will open saying that this e-mail address has been  blocked
and that all future messages from it will be blocked. It then gives you  the
option to delete all current messages from that  e-mail  address  from  your
inbox.

To view the Blocked Senders List, click on;

Tools

Message Rules

Blocked Senders List

From here you can view all blocked senders, add or remove any addresses
