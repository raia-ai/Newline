                   Inishowen Co-Op Mart Direct to Factory:

1:    WEEK 1/2/3/4

Setup Week 1/2/3/4
[pic]

Enter lots in full lot and price input
[pic]

When all lots are entered go into;

 21: Sales Totals Lot lists to  add  Remarks  and  Lorry  number  (in  grade
column)
[pic]
Note: If you have not entered any grades you will have to  go  into  5:  Lot
edit to enter one.
      You may also have to click on configuration & the day of the  week  to
select grade (this will only need to be done once!)
[pic]

Once the stock has gone off to the factory and you know the kill date  setup
the relevant ‘direct to factory’ sale before moving the lots.

2:    MOVELOTS

After the relevant ‘direct to factory’ sale is setup go into;

11: Movelot from the main menu (Note Simon is developing this  so  that  you
do not have to type in the sale it will be selectable from a list. You  will
not be able to enter stock into a closed sale and there will  be  a  ‘select
all’ option to move all lots.

In the interim period;

Enter the sale name, week 1/2/3/4 and select the relevant day  of  the  week
from the drop down menu. Select the direct sale (eg:  directs,  directH  you
are moving the stock to and the relevant day of the week.

Click on ‘get transactions’ and select all lots. F1 to move lots

When you are happy that you have finished with week 1/2/3/4  you  can  close
the sale with NL98, after this the  sale  is  ready  to  be  setup  for  the
following week.
[pic]

3:    DIRECT SALES

Enter a Price lot;

In 4: Full lot and price input, enter the seller as I102  (Inishowen  Mart).
Enter the lot as 1000, in remarks type L price H price. Enter a price  of  0
and I102 as the buyer.

Print off all bills, print off all cheques.

Close sale.

4:    AFTER SALES & HISTORY

To view factory’s history from the main menu;

Go into 1: Customer Search, enter customers name or  account  no,  click  on
buyer & view invoices from history
[pic]
Enter date range and click on search. This can be  printed  by  clicking  on
Print to.
[pic]
For sellers history go through the same process  but  choose  ‘Seller’  from
the options at the top of the customer edit screen.

To find Prices for a direct sale from the main menu go into;

5: History Analysis
7: History Search
1: Lot Number Search
      Enter date range & lot number 1000:
[pic]
