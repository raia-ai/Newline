Apply Interest in the sales ledger to overdue accounts:

Once a month select calculate interest.

Enter the date ranges you wish to apply interest charges over. The to date will be 14 or 15 days back from today.

Enter the annual percentage charge to apply.

Save and apply the charges by clicking save and update ledger.

Calc:

((No of days of debt * % rate) * value of debt / 100) / 365

Example

((23 days debt * 5) * 714 / 100) /365 = 2.25
