Including/Excluding Charges to Customers

1. Setup Relevant Charges:

From the main menu;

    • Option 7:   Customer Groups & Contact Management
[pic]

    • Option 6:   Setup Mailing List Codes
[pic]

    • Double click on code that you are going to create your charge in.
[pic]

    • Type in the description for the charge and press return
    • Click on Save and Exit when finished adding charges.

2. Allocate Charges to Customers:

From the main menu;

    • Option 1:   Customer Search
    • Enter customers name, number or herd number
    • Click on the add button next to Customer Groups
[pic]

    • Click on charge from Customer Groups Available and then click  on  the
      arrow pointing to the right
[pic]
    • It will appear in the Registered Groups, click ok. It will now  appear
      in the list of Customer Groups in the view/amend customer screen.

3. Allocate Inclusion\Exclusion to a Charge

From the main menu;

    • Option 10:  Access current sale
    • Select sale
    • Option 26:  Setup Menu
    • Option 4:   Amend Charge, Deductions and Buyer Commission
    • Option 1:   Amend Charge for item types
    • Double click on the item type you want to add the charge to.
    • On the relevant charge choose either  charge  for  Customer  Group  or
      exclude Customer Group, select the relevant mailing type that you have
      created.
[pic]
    • Click on save, it will give you the option to apply these  changes  to
      all item types in that alpha character.
    • When finished click on save and exit.
