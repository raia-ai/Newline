Buyer Cheques:

Buyers cheque taken in payment during the sales can be entered into NAS during a sale. There has been no historical front-end option to view these however until now (Feb 2017).

History:

Select the history analysis menu option.

Select sale totals.

Double click a year and select a sale. Note it does not have to be the sale the cheque was recorded in.

From the options menu select view buyers cheques.

Searching

Often the reason for searching for one of these cheques is that it has been returned from the bank. You may have very little information about it. You may not know which sale it relates to. There are a number of ways you can search for the cheque information.

Click the search by option you require.

You may wish to search by sale date but you can leave this blank.

Reference No

To search by cheque number tick the option button and enter the number. Click continue to find matches.

Amount

To search by amount click the amount option, type the amount and click continue.

Customer No

To search by the customer computer account number click the customer acc no option, enter the number and click continue.

Results

On clicking continue the results are populated into the grid below.

Output

The grid data may be output to a csv \ excel style file or printed out.
