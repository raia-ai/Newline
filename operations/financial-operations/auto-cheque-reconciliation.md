Your bank should be able to provide you with an electronic  bank  statement.
This will allow you to  download  a  file  from  the  bank  and  use  it  to
reconcile your cheques in a matter of minutes.

Browse to the file downloaded from the bank.

[pic]

You will see a list of matched cheques.

[pic]

You may see cheques not found in the bank file.
