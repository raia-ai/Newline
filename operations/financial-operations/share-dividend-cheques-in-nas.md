NAS Share Dividend System:

NAS has a basic share dividend system which allows the number of shares and share number to be saved against customer accounts. There is then a facility to set the dividend rate and produce dividend cheques from the system.

Customer Settings:

Dividend Cheque Production:

In the main menu in NAS see the menu options top left of the screen. Look in the Add-ins menu and Share Certificates.

Tick the Share Holder Customer Group and click the continue button. Enter the dividend rate and which bank account the cheques will be produced from in NAS (A-Z).

This brings back a list of all matching customers.

There are menu options

The user may print a list of share members. Print share dividend cheques based on the share dividend rate and number of shares stored on the customer account. Post the cheques to history.
