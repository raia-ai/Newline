View Contacts

[pic]

Click the View Contact Details Button to View the Contacts  that  have  been
added to the database

Tick the Type of contact you want to view. E.g. Phone. Click the  Rep  whose
contacts you wish to view. In the example here S Jones is ticked(selected).

[pic]

Select the Date Range and click the Query button. The results are  displayed
in the grid below. Double clicking the grid row will display a further  grid
with a breakdown of the results. Double  Clicking  the  2nd  grid  row  will
display the individual contact details.

To print the contacts click the print button. This can be used as  a  follow
up list for the reps.

[pic]

View Contact Details to Follow Up

The Same procedure applies here as to View Contact Details, except  for  the
initial button to click is called View Contact Details to Follow Up.
