View Cash Credits in history

Customers/Sellers who are due to be paid cash for their sold goods will
appear on the sales ledger as a credit.

[pic]

Double clicking the credit record row will load the detailed breakdown of
the sellers record. Double clicking the row within this screen will load
the copy cheque routine and show the user the details of the money to be
paid.

[pic] [pic]

Quick Cash Payments in the Cheques Option

Call up the seller in question and press the C for All Cash option if you
wish to pay the full amount in cash.
