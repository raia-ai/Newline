Market Totals & Sale Sheet Call Through


From the During the Sale Menu pick option 18. Sale Totals.

[pic]

You are shown all the  sections  for  which  lots  have  been  entered.  The
numbers of items entered and sold and the total value  of  the  section  and
the sale total. For details of the item types sold within the section  arrow
up or down the grid and press the enter key when the  section  you  want  is
highlighted.

[pic]

The are also right click options that provide lot details  by  section.  The
right click option provide sale sheet call through options.  F8  closes  the
second grid.

To view individual item(animal) types right click the row you  want  on  the
2nd grid and click the Individual Lots option.
