To see a Buyers Ledger Record select menu option 4. Sales Ledger. Menu
option 5 See Buyers Record.

[pic]

[pic]

The system defaults to Outstanding Debtors onlt but the user may select All
by clicking in the option box.

[pic]

The user may enter the buyer account number and press enter.

[pic]

Or
Enter the user name and press enter.

[pic]

The name search will show matching results. Either arrow up and down the
list and press enter when the one you need is highlighted or double click
the row in question.

[pic]

You will then see the buyers details.

[pic]

[pic]

Filter options are available in the Options menu at the top left of the
screen. Select the options desired and press the Apply Filters button.

[pic]

Printing:
Click the print button and select your printer.

[pic]

You may print it out in Landscape or portrait.

[pic]

You can preview it before you print it.

[pic]
