ALTER THE VAT RATE IN NAS

You MUST set VAT rate in each sale (at all sites and on all laptop servers), to alter it select
10 Auction System.




Click number 3 View Sale Settings (If you do not have this option your system is out-of-date
and you need to contact Newline for an update)




Select the sale and work through all the sales.




Click Amend Sale Settings




Click the Options Menu.




Click the VAT RATE option from the options menu.




Amend to 15 and press OK. Do not add a % sign.
