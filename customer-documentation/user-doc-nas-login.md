INTRO:

Newline have a new login screen for NAS.

BENEFITS:

Shows out of hours support details

Provides better automated deployment and remote installation update solutions

Traffic light system to show user licence usage

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Double click the NAS icon and enter your username and password to log into NAS.

The user licence information is shown on the top left of the screen. A green traffic light shows you are within user licence agreed limits. Amber shows you are slightly over agreed limits. Red shows you are way in excess of agreed limits.

At the bottom of the screen the user may click the support details to get out of hour contact details on any given day Monday to Saturday.

When there are program updates for NAS the user will see a list of updates and may choose to accept them by clicking the green ok button, or click the cancel button to not accept the updates at the time.
