Please see the checklist to ensure these tasks have  been  addressed  before
Newline attend the site for training and going live.


   1. AIM Webservices Agreement Live System document
   2. Commission Rates
   3. Stationary
   4. Broadband

   1. AIM Webservices Agreement Live System document
   The Department of Agriculture and Food will send you a document that  you
   must complete and return to them before  they  will  release  a  security
   certificate that allows you to connect to their online database.
