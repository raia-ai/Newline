To install the Allflex Data Manager program insert the  CD  into  the  CDROM
drive. The CD will auto-run and you will the message box shown in figure 1:

[pic]

Figure 1


Click the OK button and DCOM98 will be installed on your machine. This is  a
patch required by all VB programs using Access Database  tables.  A  message
box will then prompt you to restart your computer.  Click  the  Yes  button.
See figure 2:

[pic]

Figure 2


When your PC reboots, MDAC20 and MDAC21 will be installed  on  your  PC  and
you will be prompted to reboot again. Click Yes. See figure 3:

[pic]

Figure 3









You have now installed the necessary Microsoft patches  and  must  begin  to
install the VB program. Follow the instructions on screen.  Click  the  Next
button as demonstrated in figure 4:

[pic]

Figure 4



























The  installation  program  will  create  a  new  directory.   The   default
destination is C:\Allflex. Do not change it, just click next.

[pic]

Figure 5

Pick the Typical option and click next. See figure 6:

[pic]

Figure 6


Click next.

[pic]

Figure 7



[pic]

Figure 8


The rest of the program installs automatically. The progress bar  will  move
from 0% to 100% as shown in figure 9:

[pic]

Figure 9


























You will then be prompted to reboot  the  system.  Do  so  by  clicking  the
Finish button.

[pic]
