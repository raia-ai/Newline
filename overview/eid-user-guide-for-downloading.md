Sheep EID: USER GUIDE (Download – Tag-ie Stick)


Using the stick:


Press the GREEN ON button on the stick.
[pic]

Please Note: On turning on the display will show the current battery level.

Wait until the ‘Pen’, ‘ Buy’, ‘Anim’ tags have appeared on the screen.
[pic]

Click on the RED PEN NO button
[pic]

Enter the Pen number (in the example, below, we have entered Pen No 10)  and
press GREEN OK button
If you need to enter a sub lot enter it at this point.
[pic]

PLEASE NOTE: the letters are A-J on the number pad;
1=A
2=B
3=C
4=D
5=E
6=F
7=G
8=H
9=I
0=J

Click GREEN OK button and now press the trigger to read  the  sheep  in  the
pen. Each successful read the end of the stick will flash BLUE. [pic]
If you read the same tag twice it will flash BLUE & RED &
[pic]
‘Exists in Pen’ appears on the display.
[pic]

When you have finished with the pen click on the RED PEN NO button to  begin
the next pen and go through the same process as before.


Moving Tags from one Pen to another:

If you read the same tag in a different Pen the display will say ‘Exists in
Pen..’
[pic]
The display will automatically then show ‘Move here’ to accept the move
press the KG button, this will then move the TAG read in a previous pen to
this pen

Downloading into the market system:

Bring the stick back into the office and close to the computer with the
BLUETOOTH connection to the stick, ensure the stick is turned on. Go into
the sale you are working in and into option

7:    EID System Menu
[pic]

4:    Download EID Tags from Handheld Reader
[pic]

Click on Direct from Stick
[pic]

A message saying opening Comm Ports will appear, then the following warning
will appear.
[pic]

When it has read the Tags into the market system the following message box
appears.
[pic]

The Stick display also shows ‘Download Complete’
[pic]

A grid with the Pen numbers and individual tags then appears, click update
to update this information into the system.
[pic]

The Sheep Pen reading Grid then appears showing the tally between EID tags
read per pen and number of heads per pen entered into the back office
system.
[pic]

The PENS in BLUE indicate TAGS read in the EID sys-
tem, the total number does not tally with the market system  (or  stock  has
not been booked into the market system at this time).  If  stock  is  to  be
entered into the market system after the sheep TAGS have been read  go  into
the above option all the PENS should go GREEN. Any PENS still blue  indicate
a discrepancy between what has been read in  the  yard  and  what  has  been
entered into the market system.

BUYERS EID PRINTOUTS:

From the sale day menu;
Option 17.  Buyers Invoices/Reports
Option 1.   Buyers Invoices/Lists
Click on ‘Options’ select ‘Printer’ then ‘Sheep Ear Tag  Printer’  &  choose
the appropriate printer
[pic]

When you produce an Invoice for the relevant customer the system  will  Auto
print the EAR TAG print out. To not print the EAR TAG print out  change  the
Y to an N under the column Auto Print EID Tags

[pic]


SELLERS EID PRINTOUTS:

From the sale day menu;
Option 10.  Sellers Statements/Reports
Option 1.   Sellers Statements/Cheques
Click on ‘Options’ select ‘Printer’ then ‘Sheep Ear Tag  Printer’  &  choose
the appropriate printer (as shown in the image above).

To print out the sellers EID TAGS go into the statement for the  seller  and
select [E] Print EID Sheep Tags.
[pic]

Configuring the Stick to the market system:

7:    EID System Menu
[pic]

4:    Download EID Tags from Handheld Reader
