INTRO:

To text from NAS you need to setup an account with TextLocal from within NAS.

Tech Note: Requires .net 4 installed on server.

Exes – ConfigureSMSService; SMSReport; regbuyer

Sysinfo - USING_NEW_TEXTING_SERVICE (Makes purchase button visible); Text_Prices_Default (option in cust edit); FULLCONTACTSYSTEM (logs details in contact management).

Register with TextLocal via NAS for account details

From the main menu in NAS click the Contact Management option.

If setup by Newline you will see option 7. Texting Reports.

Click the click here to purchase button to load the login or new account option.

Click sign up for a free account.

Enter your user details and click create account.

A message will tell you an email has been set to you.

Go to your email and follow the link.

Enter your account details.

Click Activate my account.

Get in touch with Newline and let them know the username (email address you’ve registered with) and password you selected. Newline will then set this up for you.

Enter the clients email address they used to register with and the password and click save.

You are then able to go and buy text credits here.

Login into TextLocal by entering your email address / username and password.

Click the Buy button.

The user then enters credit card details and selects the bundle and rate they opt for. Note below these rates are an example screen shot. Newline expects these to be closer to 3p.

Change Sender From Name:

From the textlocal control panel go into all settings

Click on Sender Names

Add a new one and set as default. Can only be 11 chars long.
