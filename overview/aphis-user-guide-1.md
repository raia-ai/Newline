Aphis Sheep Movements  - Ver .255 Error Reporting

If the number of heads in a particular lot differs from the number of tags scanned the lot in question is highlighted in blue and none of the lots against that particular GDoc number are selected to send.

After sending to Aphis any lots that are rejected will be highligted in red.

Doubling clicking on the red cell will bring up the relevant error.

Showing you the exact issue reported by Aphis along with an option to print.
