The CMMS Amendments option loads a program that allows the user to produce
a CMMS amendments form.

[pic]

Double click your username.

[pic]

Enter the eartag or other data you are using to search for  the  animal  you
are looking for and press the enter key.

[pic]

Double click the row or select the row  and  press  enter  to  populate  the
amendments form.

[pic]

Click the continue button or pres the F5 key.

[pic]

Double click the printer  you  want  and  press  the  continue  button.  The
amendment form(s) will then be printed.

NB. If you are amending a buyers herd the  system  will  show  you  all  the
animals that buyer had so you can produce forms  for  all  of  them  in  one
quick hit.
