Intro

This document explains how to uninstall & update the MQL rep booking app from the Play Store.

Google Account

You need a google account to log into the Play Store. If you click the Play Store app without a google account set up on the tablet you will be prompted.

Sign in if you have an account or create a new one if you do not.

Follow the onscreen instructions to create the account.

Uninstall App from tablet

Before installing an updated version of the tablet software you need to uninstall the version on the tablet. Click into Apps and then Play Store.

Browse for Newline Bookings and click uninstall.

Update App from Play Store

How to download and update latest version from the play store

You can install Client App from the google Play Store on the tablet.

You can find the app in the “Apps” folder on the home screen. (Shown below.) Then click Play Store

Browse for Newline Bookings.

If it is already installed it shows installed and provides an uninstall option. Uninstall it.

The version of the tablet app is shown in the bottom left.

Once uninstalled you have an install button to update.

Latest app

You can see the version and date of the latest app released.
