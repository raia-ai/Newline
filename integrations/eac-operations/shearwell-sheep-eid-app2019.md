                               Shearwell Stick & App Download
       You will require:
          • Shearwell RFID Stick Reader
          • Android/iOS mobile device with the Shearwell Counter App

       Download the Shearwell ‘Sheep Counter’ app from the app store (Android and/or iOS).




       Turn on the Shearwell RFID Stick Reader (ensure the batteries are fully charged); go into the BLUETOOTH
       settings on the phone/tablet and pair the shearwell stick with the phone:




       Open the app; click on Scan Animals
