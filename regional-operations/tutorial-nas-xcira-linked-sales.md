NAS & Xcira Linked Sales

Online bidding is available at sales via Xcira. For on the fly integration between NAS and Xcira follow the instructions.

Setup a sale within NAS for Xcira

Note do NOT set up the Xcira online sale using RingManager. It will NOT link with NAS if you do this.

With the sale menu select the input option. Click the cogs in the top right-hand side of the screen.

Select Setup Xcira Sales.

Tick the section you are selling online and untick any pre-entered item types. These will up uploaded to Xcira separately to on the fly lots. Check the dates are correct. Amend if required and click the setup sale button.

Delete Sale from Xcira

In the place you setup a sale, a delete sale from Xcira button is shown if a sale is live \ active on Xcira. If you wish to delete the event click the button.

Sell On The Fly (OTF)

When the Send to Xcira button is clicked or has focus and enter button pressed the data is sent to Xcira. The Clerk will see a flashing red text saying “New Inventory”. They click Next Lot to send that lot to the marquee.

The info is sent to the online viewing screen.

The Send To Xcira button is green once the lot has been sent to Xcira. The ring clerk continues to enter price and buyer.
