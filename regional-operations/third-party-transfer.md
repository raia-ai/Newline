Report Third Party Buying Agents to AIM in Ireland

When doing a lot transfer in the buyers invoice the system can be set to prompt a message about third party buyer. This provides an alternative to recording this in the movement form in the invoice process.

When doing the movement form it will show N third party agent.

This third-party record can be seen in the buyer transfers report.
