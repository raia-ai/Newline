From the vendor and or purchaser stock analysis program select options  that
you require regarding vendor/purchaser,  market  name  and  stock  type  and
click the Run Analysis button.

At the bottom of the list of matching results you will see an email button.

 
Click the email button to show a list of customers  from  the  results  that
have email addresses saved against their computer account.

[pic]

If the user clicks the email button and  there  are  no  matching  addresses
they will see a message to tell  them  there  are  no  email  addresses  for
customers in the results list.
 
[pic]
 
If there are matches they will see a form to enter an email subject  message
and a longer message. See form below:
[pic]

When you are happy with the message click the Send Email  button.  The  user
is asked what sort of email program they normally use to send email.  If  it
is Outlook they should click yes. Note:  Outlook  is  different  to  Outlook
Express. Click the No button if you use  Outlook  Express  or  do  not  have
email installed on the PC you are  running  the  option  from.  Please  note
attachment option is available for  Non  Outlook  users.  Click  the  browse
button and find the file you wish to attach before clicking the  Send  Email
button.

[pic]
 
Outlook Users

Outlook users will view message in web outlook format  and  make  insertions
or BCC, etc.
 
[pic]



 Non Outlook Users

Users that click No will be asked what email  address  customers  can  reply
to. Enter the email address you expect people to send  emails  back  to  you
upon.
 
 
[pic]
 
 The status of the connection and email address are  checked  and  validated
and reported in the status list. You will  then  either  get  a  success  or
failure message. If the message reports a failure please  check  your  email
addresses are ok.
[pic]
