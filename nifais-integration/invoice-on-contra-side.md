When in the invoice screen for any given buyer and upon entering I for
invoice, if the buyer has also sold at the sale the user will see a C for
Contra (highlighted in Red)  option available in the bottom left of the
screen.

[pic]

Enter C and press the enter key.

[pic]

The system will work out the possible contra and display a confirmation
screen.

[pic]

Press F5 or click the F5 Save button to save the contra. No details will be
displayed on the bill, but when you produce the cheque the contra will be
knocked off automatically.

If the full amount is taken select the unpaid option

[pic]

If the invoice was part paid by the contra and you wish to pay the rest of
select P for paid and pay off the remaining debt.
