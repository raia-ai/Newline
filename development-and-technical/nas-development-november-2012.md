Customer Edit

It is a legal requirement in the EU to have a letter of engagement signed by your customers. The LOE Date field in the customer edit screen allows you to store a record of when the customer signed the LOE.

Customer Lists

There a is a new option to produce a report showing which customers have signed a LOE.

Bank Reconciliation

There is an update to the cheque reconciliation option to reconcile the entire bank both credits and debits. It enables reconciliation of multiple bank accounts. Separate documentation is available.
